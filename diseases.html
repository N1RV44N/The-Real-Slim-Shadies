Types Of Diseases

There are many heart diseases out there, & here are a few:
Coronary Artery Disease. Symptoms include: Variety from no symptoms, to chest pain, to a heart attack.
High Blood Pressure. Symptoms include: No symptoms (usually). If not treated, can lead to health diseases such as: strokes & different heart diseases.
Cardiac Arrest. Symptoms include: loss of consciousness and unresposiveness, severe cases can lead to death.

Causes for Heart Diseases

A few causes for heart diseases are:
Diabetes: Diabetes in simple terms, is elevated levels of blood sugar, which can be caused by eating exccessive red meat and/or sugar
Drug misuse: When prescription and/or illegal drugs are overdosed on
Emotional stress: Stress at work, school, etc.
Excessive use of alcohol and/or caffeine: Overdrinking alcohol and/or caffeine. QUIT ALCOHOL!!
High blood pressure: Control your blood pressure by eating less fatty foods and less sugar.
Smoking: Smoking ciggarettes can cause cancer. QUIT SMOKING!!
